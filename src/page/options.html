‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add custom keywords?

+

You can now add custom keywords! Go to the "Custom Keywords" section above and enter your keyword, website URL, optional search parameter, and description. Your custom keywords will work just like the built-in ones.

What if a keyword doesn't work?

+

Make sure you're typing the keyword correctly. Check the table above for all available keywords and their usage examples.

How do I customize background images?

+

Use the "Background Image Preferences" section above to add, edit, or remove Unsplash search queries. These queries determine what types of images are used for your background. You can add terms like "architecture", "minimalist", "ocean", etc.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

+

You can send feedback by clicking the "Feedback" link in the main interface or email us directly.
